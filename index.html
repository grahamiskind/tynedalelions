Ordinary People

We are a group of men and women, from all walks of life, who freely give of their time to help local charities, good causes and deserving individuals. We raise tens of thousands of pounds every year and help many people in the community, and have fun doing it!

Registered Charity

We are a registered charity and part of Lions International, one of the world’s largest service organisations. While we focus on serving the local community, we also contribute to life-saving national and international projects.

Long History

Formed in 1977, the Club meets at the County Hotel in Hexham. Meetings commence at 7.45pm on the second and fourth Mondays in the month (except for bank holidays). We also organise regular social activities for our members such as days out and meals.

Lions Clubs
Tynedale Lions Club is part of the global organisation Lions Clubs International. Our 1.4 million members – who perform community service in 210 countries and geographic areas – are different in many ways, but share a core belief: community is what we make it. Tynedale Lions are part of a network of clubs in the North East and Scotland (District NE105). We support each other in our activities and promote the creation of new clubs. We have two daughter clubs at Mid Tyne based in Ryton, Tyne and Wear and at Allendale, Northumberland.

Join Us

Club members are all volunteers giving their time to run events that raise money to help those less fortunate than themselves whilst enjoying fun and fellowship. They meet regularly to plan their fund raising and social events and to agree which of the many requests for help they receive are most worthy of support from the limited funds they have available.

Our current President, Lion Jonathan Hill is committed to building up the strength of the Club’s membership and continuing to support a wide range of projects in the local community not just by providing funds but increasingly by providing direct support for individuals and groups.
If you would like to join us, call Jonathan on 0845 833 7359 or Ian on 01434 605936 or email us at tynedalelionsclub@gmail.com

Apply for Grants!

Tynedale Lions are pleased to consider all applications for funding from organisations or individuals. We particularly support applications which benefit the Tynedale area. Many organisations and individuals have beenfited from our fund raising over the years, from large charirties (e.g., Teenage Cancer Trust, Great North Air Ambulance) all the way through to individuals seeking help. 

Our Santa's sleigh and annual Beer Festival raise thousands of pounds for good causes that we give out throughout the year so anytime is a good time to apply.
